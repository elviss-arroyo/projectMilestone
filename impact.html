The impact of HTTP is huge. It has changed how we live, work, and learn:

  • Social Media: HTTP makes Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, and TikTok possible, connecting people all over the world.
  • Business & E-commerce: Companies like Amazon and eBay sell stuff online. People can shop from home.
  • Online Learning: Websites like Khan Academy, Coursera, and YouTube let people learn new things on the internet.
  • Information Sharing: HTTP makes it easy to find info fast, like a giant online library.
  • Security: A negative impact of HTTP is security issues which was later resolved with HTTPS.

In short, HTTP changed almost everything in modern life, connecting people, info, and businesses in new ways.
